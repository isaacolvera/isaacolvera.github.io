Smart CCTV Camera

A python based project that uses an external camera to record and take pictures of detected bodies and faces that appear within frame. All videos are saved on the working PC where the program is running, and the pictures are sent to an authorized email to act as an alert for the user.

Technologies that I used was Python, my laptop, an external camera, and a Google account to store the photos taken

Challenges: There was two main challenges that I faced when creaing this project. The first one was sending the photos/videos to the Google account, and the second was understanding how to properly use OpenCV on Python. A lot of times the camera would not properly detect a body or face so I spent a lot of time making sure those parameters were set correctly

Outcome: A fully function security program that detects bodies/faces, records videos, saves the video to the user's laptop, and sends photos to the user's google account
